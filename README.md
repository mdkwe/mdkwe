## About me

I'm an energy engineer based in Paris, with a background in process engineering and energy economics.

I’m also a self-taught developer. I started coding in 2023 through [The Hacking Project](https://www.thehackingproject.org/), then kept learning independently by building projects.

My work is driven by a growing interest in the role of data and software in making energy systems easier to understand, design, and navigate.

<p align="align">
  Right now, I’m building <strong>Papaya</strong> — a structured database of energy data sources designed to help people find, assess, and use the right energy information faster.
</p>

<p align="align">
  Long term, I want to contribute to more locally adapted energy systems by strengthening the information layer that supports better design, financing, and infrastructure development, especially in underserved regions.
</p>
